Multi-Agent Systems in 3D Virtual Worlds: A Comprehensive Study

Hao Wang, Cheng Chong, Zhang Zheng

Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) 2024

PDF Code Project Page Slides

Abstract

This paper presents a comprehensive study on multi-agent systems operating in 3D virtual worlds. We explore various coordination strategies and demonstrate significant improvements in task performance. Our approach combines reinforcement learning with hierarchical planning to enable agents to collaborate effectively in complex environments.
